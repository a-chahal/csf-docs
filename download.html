How to Install

1

Download the APK

Tap the download button above on your Android device. If prompted, choose "Keep" or "Download anyway."

2

Open the file

Open your Downloads folder or tap the download notification, then tap the APK file.

3

Allow installation

Android will ask you to allow installing from this source. Tap "Settings" then toggle on "Allow from this source," then tap "Install."

4

Open MyCSF

Once installed, tap "Open" or find MyCSF in your app drawer. Create an account and get started!

Why am I installing outside the Play Store? MyCSF is currently being reviewed for the Google Play Store. In the meantime, this is the same signed release build that will be on the Play Store. Once approved, you'll be able to update directly from Google Play.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe to install?

Yes. This APK is signed with the same official release key used for the Google Play Store. It has not been modified or tampered with.

Will I lose my data when the Play Store version is available?

No. Since both builds use the same signing key and package name, the Play Store update will seamlessly replace this version and keep all your data.

What Android version do I need?

Android 7.0 (Nougat) or newer. This covers the vast majority of Android devices in use today.

I see a "Blocked by Play Protect" warning. What do I do?

Tap "More details" then "Install anyway." This warning appears for any app not yet listed on the Play Store and is normal for direct downloads.
