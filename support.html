Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log volunteer hours?

Navigate to the Hours tab, tap the "+" button, and fill in the event details including the date, event name, and hours completed. You can optionally add an organizer signature for verification.

How do I check in to a meeting?

There are two ways to check in:

  • QR Code: Tap "Scan QR" on the Attendance tab and scan the meeting QR code displayed by an officer
  • Bluetooth: If enabled, the app will automatically detect nearby meeting beacons when you're at the meeting location

How do I edit my profile?

Go to the Profile tab and tap "Edit Profile" to update your information including name, phone number, and grade level.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile tab and tap "Delete Account" at the bottom. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

I forgot my password

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a code to reset your password.
